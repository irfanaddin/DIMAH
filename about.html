About Us

At Dimah Private Security, we are committed to delivering professional, reliable, and trusted security solutions that protect what matters most to you.
With years of industry experience, our team of licensed and insured security professionals provides 24/7 protection tailored to meet the unique needs of each client—whether for commercial, residential, or personal security.
We take pride in being more than a security provider—we are your safety partner. Working hand-in-hand with your management team, we strive to continually improve your security program through a proactive, customized approach.
Our full-service security management includes uniformed security officers, consulting services, and comprehensive all-hazard risk assessments.
Every DPS officer is a carefully selected ambassador of our company, chosen through rigorous independent background checks, narcotics screening, and human resources evaluations. This ensures that each member of our team upholds the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and vigilance.
