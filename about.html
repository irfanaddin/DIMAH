About Us

At Dimah Private Security, we are committed to delivering professional, reliable, and trusted security solutions that protect what matters most to you.
With years of industry experience, our team of licensed and insured security professionals provides 24/7 protection tailored to meet the unique needs of each client—whether for commercial, residential, or personal security.
We take pride in being more than a security provider—we are your safety partner. We work hand-in-hand with your management team to continually improve your security program through a proactive, customized approach.
Every DPS officer is a carefully selected ambassador of our company, chosen through rigorous independent background checks, narcotics screening, and human resources evaluations.
